ABOUT NATHIA GALI

Nathia Gali is a popular hill station and scenic tourist destination located in the Abbottabad District of the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province of Pakistan. It is part of the Galyat range, a series of picturesque hill stations in the Himalayan foothills, and it is situated about 80 kilometers (50 miles) from Islamabad, the capital of Pakistan. Nathia Gali is renowned for its stunning natural beauty. The town is nestled in the midst of lush green hills and thick forests of pine and oak trees. The scenic landscapes, cool climate, and fresh mountain air make it a perfect escape from the heat and hustle of the cities. The town enjoys a temperate climate, with cool summers and snowy winters. Nathia Gali is a haven for nature enthusiasts and trekkers. It offers several trekking trails and hiking opportunities, such as the Mukshpuri and Miranjani treks, which lead to breathtaking viewpoints with panoramic views of the surrounding valleys and peaks. While in Nathia Gali, you can savor the local cuisine, which often includes delicious dishes made from fresh mountain produce and traditional Pakistani flavors.
